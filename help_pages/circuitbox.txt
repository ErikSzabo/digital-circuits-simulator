CircuitBox

With this tool, you can put your whole circuit to a box. And the reason to that, well, you can save and load
your boxes. So next time if you want to use the same circuit, the only thing you need is to load your box.
Don't bind switch pins to input pins! You won't be able to interact with the switch after loading.(on off methods)

Following methods are only work in Box Editor Mode. For that, type: boxeditor

    Create a box: "box <name> create <input pin number> <output pin number>"

    Bind your device pins to the box:
        - To bind an input pin, type:
             "box <box name> bindinput <target device name> <target pin index> <box pin index>"
        - To bind an output pin, type:
             "box <box name> bindoutput <target device name> <target pin index> <box pin index>"

    After exiting Box Editor Mode, your boxes will be saved and added to your normal mode.

Following methods are works in normal mode as well:

    Load CircuitBox
        type: "device <boxname> load"
        After loading, you can connect your box as usual.
